Add Modpack Sources

A step by step guid on how to add Modpack Sources to Matix's Mod Installer.

1. Step

Open the Options by clicking on the options button on the top left.

2. Step

Inside of the options window click on edit modpack sources.

3. Step

Inside of the modpack sources window click on add.

4. Step

Paste the new modpack source and click on OK.

5. Step

Now click on save and close the option window.
